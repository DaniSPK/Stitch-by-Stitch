About Us

Stitch by Stitch is a local South Florida business started by a young entrepreneur who one day wishes to be a fashion designer. Our owner’s first contact with a sewing machine was in first grade, when she made her costume for a play she was participating in at her school. Later on, she had classes with a friend’s grandmother and later had classes at Petite Designers™ with another friend...

Our Mission

Our mission is to create the most comfortable and reusable, fabric based handmade products for you, the person you help by buying a mask and most importantly, safe for the environment. In the pandemic that we are living through, our planet has suffered greatly with our careless disposing of plastic masks and other utensils that help us not spread covid.
